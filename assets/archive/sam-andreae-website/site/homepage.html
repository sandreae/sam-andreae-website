Over the last 6 months I've been working on a new compositional method which use colour, along with shape and position, to suggest parameter changes, performer, or gesture type. The focus with these pieces is connected events (musical or otherwise) and structural spontaneity within a group performance. I've done 2 performances (In Berlin and London) along with a workshop for students on the Rock and Pop course at the RNCM in Manchester. Good feedback all round, the workshops were particularily fun, and I hope to record/video performances soon.

DECEMBER 2014
The last few months have been dominated (in the best way possible) by mine and Dave Birchalls Massive Monochord residency at the Penthouse in Manchester. We had such an incredible time developing the project and the opportunity to spend an extended period of focussed exploration yielded some really nice results. Thanks to everyone who came to the workshop and concert (audience and performers!) and of course to Rosanne and Debbie for inviting us to invade their space for two months ;-)


More fun with Rodrigo Constanzo and his Dfs (Dynamic Score) composition system for improvisors (Anton Hunter, Linda Jankowska, PA Trembley, Alex Harker and Richard Craig). I wrote a piece using the software and its ace, great possibilities.

ABC were also off on a short tour in Switzerland, Silence Blossoms played in Norway and Strikethrough Me and You in London. Good times had by all.

Tonight is the debut performance of Saxoctopus (saxophone octet improv with Cath Roberts, Tom Ward, Colin Webster, Rachael Mussons, Julie Kjaer and Dee Byrne) at The Vortex in London and this Thursday is the debut performance for Bird Rat Centipede (Noise Upstairs Manchester) which is a new ensemble focussing on composition with a leaning towards graphic/text scores (not exclusively). Thats it for now. HAPPY CHRISTMAS

10/02/13
Oops! It's been a few months since I updated my website, I'm going to blame my recent repatriation and move back to the UK. I'm now getting settled back in here in the North of England and all is looking bright for the future (not literally bright of course, cos I'm in the North of England....). It's good to be re-establishing old musical relations with the likes of Anton Hunter, Rodrigo Constanzo, Ben Cottrell and Johnny Hunter and forming new ones with David Birchall along with getting a small insight into the thriving Leeds music scene when I played with Dave Kane's Rabbit Orchestra at he end of last year (and the world as we know it, apparently).

I toured and recorded with Trio Riot and Silence Blossoms in November last year, so eep your eyes peeled for those releases later this year. The SB recording was in a fantastic converted barn house just outside Hathersage, we had a really special time recording and living there. Thanks to Ben Cottrell for recording us and Angela Guyton for taking some film footage for a mega video she's gunna make!! (teaser below).

Check the calendar page (newly updated) to see where I'm playing in the coming months. One gig I'm particularly looking forward to is with Seth Bennet and Corey Mwamba at the Poets and Players event in The Whitworth Art Gallery.
