Aaargh!


"Aaargh! are three brothers from different mothers whose shared love of improvised music (and Monty Python) drew them together from across the globe. Their music, described as leading the new and exciting European-wonky-melodic-folk-punk-jazz movement, mixes improvisation and composition seamlessly with each group member's personality contributing to the overall band sound."

Sam Andreae (UK) - Saxophone
Eero Tikkanen (Finland) - Bass
David Meier (Switzerland) - Drums
