Haq

Originally from England, Haq was formed in 2008 by myself and Anton Hunter. Fin Panter has always been and will be forever more our wonderful drummer and Eero Tikkanen (bass) completed the quartet in 2009. We released our debut album "Walking, Walking, Falling" on Efpi records in early 2011 which was followed shortly after by a remixes EP. We're currently working on new material and more touring is certainly in the pipelines.
